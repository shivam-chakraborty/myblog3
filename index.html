About

Recently in 2020 when I was bored in lockdown, my father brought me some kites of different kinds. At first, I was not able to fly the kites but gradually my father taught me. And now I am able to fly it on my own. Every afternoon at 5o’clock I use to go to terrace and fly a kite. Sometimes I also go out to buy kites on my own. Nowadays I have seen an uncle flying a kite and seeing him I also learnt new techniques. Although I know how to fly a kite, I don’t know how to cut down others kite.
